🌾 About KhetSeGhar

KhetSeGhar is building a transparent farm-to-society supply chain where housing societies can procure grains, pulses, and other agricultural products directly from verified Village Hubs.

🚀 Our Vision

Reinventing bulk grocery sourcing with technology and fairness.

Fair Pricing

Helping farmers receive better value by removing unnecessary intermediaries.

Direct Procurement

Connecting residential housing societies directly with producing villages.

Bulk Supply

Tailored specifically for apartment associations and society management.

Transparency

Verified agricultural inventory and trusted direct sourcing pipelines.

👨‍🌾 Platform Modules

An integrated ecosystem serving every stakeholder in the chain.

Farmer Hub

Manage crops, yields, and harvest readiness.

Village Hub

Aggregation centers handling quality checks and local logistics.

Society Hub

Order placement, bulk tracking, and payment clearance for housing committees.

Admin Control

Centralized monitoring, verification, and end-to-end management.
